Privacy Policy for FocusRPG

FocusRPG is committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

FocusRPG does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Local Storage

All data such as browsing activity, productivity statistics, XP, and settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's local storage API.

No External Tracking

FocusRPG does not use analytics, tracking scripts, or third-party data collection services.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • tabs – to detect active tab usage
  • history – to analyze browsing activity
  • idle – to detect user inactivity
  • storage – to save your data locally

Audio Features

The extension may play audio from built-in files or publicly available radio streams. No user data is transmitted during playback.

User Control

You can reset or remove all stored data by uninstalling the extension.
